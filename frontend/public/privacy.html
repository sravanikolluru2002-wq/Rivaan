Privacy Policy

Rivan Realty uses this app to help customers explore properties, schedule site visits, manage bookings, and communicate with the Rivan team. This policy explains the information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account and contact details such as name, phone number, email address, and saved profile preferences.
  • Property activity such as enquiries, scheduled visits, bookings, payment interest, and customer support requests.
  • Partner and admin work data such as assigned visits, booking status, approval status, and CRM activity.
  • Device and technical information needed for authentication, security, diagnostics, and app reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users through OTP and keep sessions active securely.
  • To show real-time property, visit, booking, notification, and dashboard updates.
  • To assign customer requests to approved partners and support the booking process.
  • To improve app stability, prevent misuse, and maintain operational audit records.

Sharing

We share relevant customer request information only with authorized Rivan admins, approved partners, and service providers required to operate the app. We do not sell personal information.

Security And Retention

We use authentication, access controls, and operational logging to protect app data. Information is retained only as long as needed for business, legal, support, and audit purposes.

Your Choices

You can request profile updates, account support, or deletion assistance by contacting the Rivan Realty support team.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Rivan Realty support through the app or at support@rivanrealty.com.
